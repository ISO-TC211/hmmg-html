A coordinate system used by an Engineering CRS. It shall be one of affine coordinate system, Cartesian coordinate system, cylindrical coordinate system, linear coordinate system, polar coordinate system, spherical coordinate system or user-defined coordinate system.<br/><br/>

Notes: A one-dimensional coordinate system that consists of the points that lie on the single axis described. Notes: A two- or three-dimensional coordinate system that consists of any combination of coordinate axes not covered by any other Coordinate System type. An example is a multilinear coordinate system which contains one coordinate axis that may have any 1-D shape which has no intersections with itself. This non-straight axis is supplemented by one or two straight axes to complete a 2 or 3 dimensional coordinate system. The non-straight axis is typically incrementally straight or curved. A UserDefinedCS shall have two or three axis associations; the number of associations shall equal the dimension of the CS.<br/>
