This class is used to list lane restrictions. Lane numbers are counted from the normal outside edge of allowable travel (rightmost in the US<br/>the Americas<br/>and most of Europe; leftmost in the UK<br/>Australia<br/>and Japan).<br/><br/>Allowed Lanes are ones from which the turn can be made both legally or physically if the vehicle has a large turning radius). The turning radii<br/>if given<br/>are ordered in the same manner as the allowed lanes<br/>and are the minimum turning radius required of a vehicle making this turn from this lane.<br/>
