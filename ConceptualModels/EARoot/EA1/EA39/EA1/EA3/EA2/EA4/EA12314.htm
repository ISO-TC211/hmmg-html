Spatial class represented by polygons. It is the object of a Praedial Servitude, representing e.g. a right-of-way or other type of area part of a given Parcel whose underlying owner has some form of restriction to its rights. Specialization of RestrictionArea CCDM class (now discontinued and substituted by OtherRegisterObject).<br/>
