A NT_Network is essentially two separate topologies. It geometric topology is the value of the association "complex." The second topology is the graph of the NT_Link and NT_Turn entities that comprise it.<br/><br/>Although the links and turns have the underlying geometry of the TP_Complex<br/>they have their own connectivity based on usable "vehicle" routes. If a link comes to a cross-roads<br/>and U-turns are allowed<br/>the there are up to 4 turns which exit that link and enter into one of the likes associated to a directed edge leaving that node<br/>including the one that reversed the incoming link. This micro topology does not have a different geometric existence from the "road" network<br/>but represents the potential traffic patterns through each of the "intersections" represented by the TP_Nodes of the associated TP_Complex.<br/><br/>{  complex.dimension() = 1  }<br/>
