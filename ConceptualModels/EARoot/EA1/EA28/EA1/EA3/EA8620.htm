"SubregisterDescription" is a subclass of "RegisterItem" that shall be used in the principal register of a hierarchical register to describe each of the affiliated subregisters. Their semantics are unchanged, but a few are subject to additional<br/>constraints or conditions specified.<br/><br/>NOTE Because a subregister is a type of register, it contains an instance of "Register" that describes itself. "SubregisterDescription" carries similar information about the subregister, but it is contained within the principal register.<br/>
