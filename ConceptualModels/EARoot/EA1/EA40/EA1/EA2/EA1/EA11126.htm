Currently, the valuation class, as well as street and building addresses, and land use, are all considered as External Registers to which LADM has externallinks. This means that during implementation, interfaces should be developed, such as to provide connection to (in this case) valuation registers, supplying them data from basic administrative units and related spatial units, in order to validate, process or perform transaction requests on e.g. fiscal use spatial units.<br/><br/>
