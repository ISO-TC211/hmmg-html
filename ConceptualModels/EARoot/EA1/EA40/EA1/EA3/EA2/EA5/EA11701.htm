This Instance Level Diagram depicts an actual "Baldio", existing in the Parish of Cavez, Municipality of Cabeceiras de Basto. It is owned and managed by a co-owners assembly (Assembleia de Compartes de Baldio), according specific legislation. The PT_Baldios right is recognized in the Constitution and special Laws. There is no register at this time, but there is a National Federation called BALADI. Each legally constituted "Assembleia" can manage one or more Parcels (PT_Baldio), within each Parish administrative unit.<br/>
