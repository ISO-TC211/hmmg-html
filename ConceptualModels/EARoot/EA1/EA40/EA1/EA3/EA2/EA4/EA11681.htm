Social Area identified within each Cadastral Sheet. Typical instances of Sheet Social Area are Public Roads serving several Parcels, or other areas of Public Domain (with no identifiable Titular). Is modeled as specialization from CCDM Serving Parcel. It is a spatial class represented by polygons.<br/>
