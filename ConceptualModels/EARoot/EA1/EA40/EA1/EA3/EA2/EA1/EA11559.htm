The Layers 1 to 3 together form the fullPartition (in 2D coordinate space), and so they should obbey to the following topologic relationships (according Meijers):<br/><br/>"The union of the geometry of all polygonal features should cover the<br/>whole area (so there are no gaps allowed), and no polygons are allowed<br/>to overlap (intersection of all interiors of the polygons is an empty set)"<br/><br/>Currently, there is no spatial Union operator defined in the Simple Features profile, so as to obtain the union of the geometry. However, the binary topological operators ST_Contains and ST_Overlaps (=False) could be useful to formalize such constraints, as exemplified by a specialization of the LA_RequiredRelationshipSpatialUnit.<br/>The valid containing area could be given by the CadastralCoverage root instance. <br/>
