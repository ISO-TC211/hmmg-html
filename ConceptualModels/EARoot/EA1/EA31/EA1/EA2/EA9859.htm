The type "MF_LocalGeometry " is a geometry in a engineering coordinate system (usually 3D). Instances of this type are used to define the local geometry of the moving object. <br/>

Notes: The attribute "origin" describes a point on the local geometry that will act as a placement point into greographic space. This point is chosen by the application usually for ease of calculations. Common choices for an origin might be center of gravity or center of footprint, which is the  projection of the center of gravity onto the surface. <br/>
