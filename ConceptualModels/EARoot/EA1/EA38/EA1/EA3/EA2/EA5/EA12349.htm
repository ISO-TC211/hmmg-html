This diagram is based on the article presented to JCL. The following text was reviewed to comply with LADM v3e.<br/><br/>"In this diagram, objects involved in the Horizontal Property relationship were included. Real Estate objects represent units in a larger (apartment) Building (PT_BuildingUnit), with the individual unit related to a specific natural person, Tomas, through the Right of Horizontal Property, registered through the Land Register (LA_RecordedObject).<br/>To fully characterize Horizontal Property, however, also the Building parts hold in common, represented by the SharedUnit (SharedUnit:PT_BuildingUnit), should be related to the group of persons holding Horizontal Property Rights on the Building, that is, the "Condo Owners Assembly" Group Person (LA_GroupParty), through a Common Parts Right."<br/>
