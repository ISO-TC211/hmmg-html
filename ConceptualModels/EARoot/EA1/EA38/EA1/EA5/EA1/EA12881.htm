Abstract class representing all the forms of ownership under Private Law. Bellow, two definitions are given.<br/>(Paasch, 2005): Ownership is a very strong right commonly connected with land and is executed by the legal owner, e.g. the government, a company or one or more private individuals, according to the legislation in the country in question;<br/>(Zevenbergen, 2004): Maximum Real Right, or strongest right available in a jurisdiction.<br/>
