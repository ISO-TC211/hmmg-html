Envelope is often referred to as a minimum bounding box or rectangle. Regardless of dimension, a Envelope can be represented without ambiguity as two direct positions (coordinate points). To encode a Envelope, it is sufficient to encode these two points. This is consistent with all of the data types in this standard, their state is represented by their publicly accessible attributes. <br/>
