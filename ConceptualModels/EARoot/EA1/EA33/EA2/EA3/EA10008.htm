The LC_AbioticElement metaobject is a subtype of LC_Element and has three subtypes representing the three basic types of abiotic surface land cover. The subtypes are: LC_ArtificialSurfaceElement, LC_NaturalSurfaceElement, and LC_WaterBodyAndAssociatedSurfaceElement.<br/><br/>
