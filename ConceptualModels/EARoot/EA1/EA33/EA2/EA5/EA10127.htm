The LC_EvenAge component is a subtype of LC_GrowthFormAge. This component may be used to refine the metalanguage object corresponding to an LC_GrowthFormAge object with respect to the age of a specific vegetative layer, where there is an even age within the growth form strata, to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS.<br/>
