This example describes two vegetation elements within one stratum. Herbs compete for space with Dwarf Shrubs. The whole form a single stratum were the sum of the cover of both elements is not more than 100%. <br/><br/>The model  shows a class EL_DwarfShrubs:LC_LandCoverClass composed of elements that correspond to instances of the metalanguage objects from LCML. The class EL_DwarfShrubs:LC_LandCoverClass has been built up with a layer of herbaceous growth with cover (70 to 90 %) and a layer of shrubs with open cover (10 to 30 %) and height (0.2 to 0.5 meters). <br/><br/>The class EL_DwarfShrubs is an instance of LC_LandCoverClass and is composed of one instance of EL_HorizontalPattern. <br/><br/>
