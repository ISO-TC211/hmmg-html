The LC_GrowthForm metaobject  is a subtype of LC_VegetationElement and has four subtypes LC_WoodyGrowthForm, LC_HerbaceousGrowthForm, LC_LichenAndMoss and LC_Algae. This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all growth form vegetation covered surfaces.<br/><br/>
