The LC_NonBuiltUpSurface metaobject is a subtype of LC_ArtificialSurfaceElement. This metaclass, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all non-built up artificial surfaces. The LC_NonBuiltUpSurface metaobject has two subtypes LC_Deposit and LC_Extraction.<br/><br/>
