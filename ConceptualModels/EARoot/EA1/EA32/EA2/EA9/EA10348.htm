The instance of EL_BouldersWithMoss:LC_HorizontalPattern is composed of three instances of EL_BouldersWithMoss:LC_Stratum. This means that the association class may be implemented in an instantiation as additional attributed on the instantiated class. This is what is shown in these examples.<br/><br/>
