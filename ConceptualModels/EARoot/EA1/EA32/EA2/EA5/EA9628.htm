The LC_CultivatedAndManagedVegetation component is a subtype of LC_VegetationArtificiality. This component may be used to refine the metaobject corresponding to an LC_GrowthFormAge class with respect to the artificiality of a specific vegetative layer to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS.  <br/>CultivatedAndManagedVegetation has eleven subtypes LC_UrbanPark, LC_CropYield, LC_Plantation, LC_CropGrowingParameter, LC_PlantSpreadingGeometry, LC_WaterSupplyPeriod, LC_FieldSize, LC_MechanicalErosionControl, LC_PestControl, LC_CropFertilization and LC_Ploughing.<br/><br/>
