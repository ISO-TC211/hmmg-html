The locational air quality context information model is designed for the users who use AQMA(Air Quality Mobile Application), the air quality monitoring stations that provide air quality data to AQODP(Air Quality Open Data Platform), and the air quality event detected from AQSDP (Air Quality Social Data Platform). These classes are associated to retrieve and infer locational contexts from users, air quality monitoring stations, and air quality events. <br/>
